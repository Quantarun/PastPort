About This Chatbot

This AI-powered chatbot embodies the economic philosophy and social reform ideas of Henry George (1839-1897), the American political economist famous for his advocacy of land value taxation and the single tax movement.

Through advanced natural language processing and a comprehensive knowledge base of George's writings, speeches, and economic theories, this chatbot offers an interactive way to explore his influential ideas on wealth inequality, land ownership, and economic justice.
